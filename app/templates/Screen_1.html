Our purpose

Imagine that you are a salesman at shell shop and get a monthly pay of Rs. 20,000. You have to manage all your expenses in this budget, which includes but is not limited to: Food, electricity bill, water bill, clothing, and other daily need items. You are living from hand to mouth. One fine day you wake up and find out that the nearest grocery shop has shifted to another town. You can’t afford traveling all the way towards it just to buy eggs. Instead, you tackle the situation smartly: you pick your cell, log on to this website and post "Hey, I need EGGS, urgency level is 4/10" and another user who is already traveling to the shop will ACCEPT your task, and will bring you eggs for a nominal fee. This way, you will save your travelling expense, and the user who was already traveling to do his task won’t travel for free. Cumulative energy is saved and time is utilized to do another productive task. One good turn deserves another
